Sizes

Here you can find out about all the different size prints we offer and our advice if you are struggling to choose. Our sizes are available in centimetres and inches and fit most standard frame sizes.

50 x 70 cm (18 x 24 Inches) – The perfect gift

As a one-off or the start of a collection, the 50 x 70 cm map is our most versatile and popular size. As there are a huge variety of fitting frames, it also makes the perfect gift. The 50 x 70 cm maps look great together in twos, threes or fours in a horizontal gallery. If you are a globetrotter this might be the perfect way to capture your most memorable places.

The most amazing use of a 50 x 70 cm map was a gallery of 30 cities in Sonoma County, California. Side by side on two rows they look fantastic.

There is almost no place on earth that won’t fit perfectly onto a 50 x 70 cm map, whether in portrait or landscape format.

61 x 91 cm (24 x 36 Inches) – Perfect in a Pair

If your favourite place is not quite wide enough to warrant a panoramic the 61 x 91 cm size could be the perfect choice as it has the highest aspect of our standard sized prints.

70 x 100 cm – Great alone

If you are looking to fill a large space with a single map, this is the size for you. The 70 x 100 cm map works perfectly alone and especially with a Handmade frame or similar frame with a wide border. Like the panoramic sizes, it attracts attention and is an unmissable statement in any room.

The 70 x 100 cm size is great in for the wall in a personal office.

Custom Sizes

If none of the above sizes works for your situation, no problem, we can also print custom sizes. If you would like to have a custom sized print, contact us with the required size in centimetres or inches. We’ll probably ask you to make a preview in our editor on one of our standard sizes so that we can get an idea of the map you’d like to create. We’ll then prepare your image in the required size and send you a link to make your order.
