PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Our most popular picture frame. Created from high-quality wood, milled with simple clean lines and presented with a satin finish. Frames are shipped fully assembled together with their print.

Note: The way the frames are sealed at the back means they are only suitable for sale with a matching sized print. If you do plan to switch the print in the future, it is possible to remove the backing. To seal it back in its original and perfect condition, framing tape is required, though not essential.
