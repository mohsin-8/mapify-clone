Holiday Order Deadlines

We do our very best to get your Christmas presents delivered in time. See below for a guide on the last chance to order to have your maps delivered in time for Christmas. 

 

If you have already missed the deadline, don’t worry we have something for you. A Mapify gift card will be delivered instantly and is designed so you can print it out at home to have something to give on the special day. More about gift cards here.

 

Note: Due to the ongoing global pandemic, shipping carriers are experiencing some disruption. It is possible some orders will take longer to arrive than expected.  We work with partners in 30 countries, to have your map produced as close to you as possible. Even with the best planning there are still chances for delays, so don’t wait too long to order your map poster.
